SONIC ARCHITECT

DJ LevenDrom is a dynamic producer and DJ hailing from Caracas, Venezuela, who has established himself as an architect of contemporary electronic sound. His music flows seamlessly between Techno, Melodic Techno, Organic House, and Deep House, weaving a sonic tapestry of organic rhythms and immersive synths.

LevenDrom's philosophy centers on creating deep, emotional grooves. His skill in fusing the raw energy of techno with the subtle warmth of organic house has earned him recognition in the scene. His single "Summer Love" is a perfect example of his signature style. He is currently pushing his sound even further with his latest project, "PROTOCOL 14-06," where he explores the bold fusion of electronic music with trap rhythms.
