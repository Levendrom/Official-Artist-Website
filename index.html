SONIC ARCHITECT

DJ LevenDrom is a dynamic producer and DJ hailing from Caracas, Venezuela, who has established himself as an architect of contemporary electronic sound. His music flows seamlessly between Techno, Melodic Techno, Organic House, and Deep House, weaving a sonic tapestry of organic rhythms and immersive synths.

He is currently pushing his sound even further with his latest project, "PROTOCOL 14-06," where he explores the bold fusion of electronic music with trap rhythms.
